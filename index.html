Gryffindor House

Gryffindor house is known for their bravery and courage. Famous Gryffindors include Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, and Ron Weasley.

Huffelpuff House

Hufflepuff house is known for their loyalty and dedication. Famous Hufflepuffs include Cedric Diggory and Newt Scamander.

Ravenclaw House

Ravenclaw house is known for valuing intelligence, wit, and wisdom. Famous Ravenclaws include Luna Lovegood and Garrick Ollivander.

Slytherin House

Slytherin house is known for valuing cunning, ambition, and resourcefulness. Famous Slytherins include Severus Snape, Bellatrix Lestrange and Voldemort (Tom Riddle).
